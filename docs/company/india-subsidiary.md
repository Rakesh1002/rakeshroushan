# Indian Subsidiary Formation Guide

## Overview

This guide covers forming RR Tech Private Limited as a wholly-owned subsidiary of RR Holdings Inc. (US Delaware C-Corp) under India's Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) regulations.

---

## FDI Route: Automatic vs Approval

### Automatic Route (Recommended)
For IT/Software services, 100% FDI is permitted under the **automatic route** - no prior RBI approval needed.

| Sector | FDI Cap | Route |
|--------|---------|-------|
| IT Services | 100% | Automatic |
| Software Development | 100% | Automatic |
| E-commerce (B2B) | 100% | Automatic |
| E-commerce (B2C Marketplace) | 100% | Automatic |
| E-commerce (B2C Inventory) | Not Allowed | - |

**Your Case**: IT/Software services → 100% automatic route ✅

## Formation Process

### Step 1: Obtain Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)

```
DSC is required for all MCA filings.

Providers:
- eMudhra (emudhra.com)
- Sify (safescrypt.com)
- NSDL (nsdl-ca.com)

Cost: ₹1,000-2,000
Validity: 2 years
Type: Class 3 (for signing)

Process:
1. Apply online
2. Submit documents (Aadhaar, PAN, Photo)
3. Video KYC verification
4. Receive DSC token (USB device) in 1-3 days
```

### Step 2: Apply for Director Identification Number (DIN)

```
DIN is a unique number for every company director in India.

Process:
1. Go to mca.gov.in
2. Fill Form DIR-3
3. Attach:
   - PAN
   - Address proof
   - Photo
4. Pay ₹500
5. DIN allotted immediately (if Aadhaar verified)
```

### Step 3: Reserve Company Name (RUN/SPICe+)

```
Name Format: RR Tech Private Limited

Rules:
- Must include "Private Limited"
- Cannot match existing company names
- Cannot include restricted words without approval

Process:
1. Apply via SPICe+ form on MCA portal
2. Propose 2 name options
3. Name approved/rejected in 2-3 days
4. Reserved for 60 days

Suggested Names:
- RR Tech Private Limited
- RR Software Private Limited
- RR Digital Private Limited
```

### Step 4: File SPICe+ (Incorporation Form)

```
SPICe+ is the integrated incorporation form that covers:
- Name reservation
- Company incorporation
- DIN allotment (for new directors)
- PAN application
- TAN application
- GST registration (optional)
- EPFO/ESIC registration (if applicable)

Documents Required:
1. MOA and AOA (INC-33 and INC-34)
2. Declaration by first directors (INC-9)
3. Consent of directors (DIR-2)
4. Address proof for registered office
5. NOC from owner (if rented)
6. Proof of stamp duty payment

Government Fees: ~₹5,000-10,000 (based on authorized capital)
```

### Step 5: Receive Certificate of Incorporation

```
After SPICe+ approval:
- Certificate of Incorporation issued
- PAN allotted
- TAN allotted
- CIN (Corporate Identity Number) assigned

Timeline: 2-5 business days after filing
```

## Post-Formation: FDI Compliance

### FCGPR Filing (Within 30 Days of Share Allotment)

When the US parent invests in the Indian subsidiary, file Form FCGPR with RBI.

```
Form: FC-GPR
Platform: FIRMS Portal (RBI)
Timeline: Within 30 days of share allotment
Documents:
- Board resolution
- Share certificates
- Valuation report (if applicable)
- KYC of foreign investor
```

### Annual Compliance (FLA Return)

```
Form: FLA (Foreign Liabilities and Assets)
Due: July 15 annually
Applies to: All companies with FDI
Platform: RBI FIRMS portal
```

## Transfer Pricing Setup

### Intercompany Agreement

Create a Master Service Agreement between:
- **RR Holdings Inc.** (US) - Service Recipient
- **RR Tech Pvt Ltd** (India) - Service Provider

```
Agreement should cover:
- Scope of services (software development, support, etc.)
- Pricing methodology (Cost Plus 15-20%)
- Invoicing frequency (monthly)
- Payment terms (Net 30)
- IP ownership (remains with US parent)
- Confidentiality
- Term and termination
```

### Monthly Invoice

```
Invoice from: RR Tech Pvt Ltd
Invoice to: RR Holdings Inc.

Description: Software development services for [Month Year]

Cost Breakdown:
- Personnel costs: ₹X
- Infrastructure costs: ₹Y
- Administrative overhead: ₹Z
- Total Cost: ₹(X+Y+Z)

Markup (15%): ₹M

Total Invoice Amount: ₹(X+Y+Z+M) + GST (18%)

Payment Terms: Wire transfer within 30 days
Bank Details: [HDFC Bank account details]
```

### GST on Services

```
Export of Services (to US parent):
- GST Rate: 0% (Zero-rated, not exempt)
- Condition: Payment received in foreign currency
- LUT Required: Letter of Undertaking for export without payment of GST

File LUT annually before April 1.
```
