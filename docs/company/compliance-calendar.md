## US Entity: RR Holdings Inc.

### Delaware State Filings

| Filing                   | Due Date | Cost         | Portal            |
| ------------------------ | -------- | ------------ | ----------------- |
| **Annual Franchise Tax** | March 1  | $400 minimum | corp.delaware.gov |
| **Annual Report**        | March 1  | $50          | corp.delaware.gov |

#### Franchise Tax Calculation

```
For companies with 10M authorized shares at $0.00001 par value:

Method 1: Authorized Shares Method
- Not favorable for high share counts

Method 2: Assumed Par Value Capital Method (Use This)
- Based on total gross assets and issued shares
- For startups: Usually $400 minimum

Payment:
1. Go to corp.delaware.gov
2. File number: [Your file number]
3. Pay online with credit card
4. Save confirmation
```

### Federal Tax Filings (IRS)

| Filing            | Due Date   | Notes                        |
| ----------------- | ---------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Form 1120**     | April 15   | Corporate income tax return  |
| **Form 7004**     | April 15   | Extension request (6 months) |
| **Extended 1120** | October 15 | If extension filed           |

#### Estimated Tax Payments

| Quarter | Due Date     | Covers         |
| ------- | ------------ | -------------- |
| Q1      | April 15     | Jan 1 - Mar 31 |
| Q2      | June 15      | Apr 1 - May 31 |
| Q3      | September 15 | Jun 1 - Aug 31 |
| Q4      | December 15  | Sep 1 - Dec 31 |

```
Required if: Expected tax liability > $500

Calculation:
- Estimate annual profit
- Calculate 21% corporate tax
- Divide by 4
- Pay quarterly

Penalty for underpayment if you pay less than:
- 100% of prior year tax, OR
- 90% of current year tax
```

### Other US Filings

| Filing                  | Due Date                    | Trigger                                        |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Form 5472**           | With 1120                   | If 25%+ foreign ownership (N/A for you)        |
| **FinCEN BOI**          | Within 90 days of formation | New requirement 2024+                          |
| **1099-NEC**            | January 31                  | If paying US contractors $600+                 |
| **State Nexus Filings** | Varies                      | If you have employees/presence in other states |

### FinCEN Beneficial Ownership Information (BOI)

```
New requirement as of 2024:

Who files: All US corporations (with exceptions)
Due: Within 90 days of formation (new companies)
      or by January 1, 2025 (existing companies)

Information required:
- Beneficial owner name
- Date of birth
- Address
- ID document (passport/driver's license)

Portal: boiefiling.fincen.gov
Cost: Free
Penalty: $500/day for non-compliance
```

#### ROC Filing Details

**AOC-4 (Financial Statements)**

```
Due: Within 30 days of AGM (AGM by September 30)
     Practically: October 29

Contents:
- Balance Sheet
- Profit & Loss Statement
- Cash Flow Statement
- Notes to Accounts
- Auditor's Report

Attachments:
- DIR-8 (Director's declaration)
- Optional attachments

Fee: Based on authorized capital
     ₹1L capital = ₹200
```

**MGT-7 (Annual Return)**

```
Due: Within 60 days of AGM
     Practically: November 29

Contents:
- Company details
- Share capital structure
- Shareholding pattern
- Directors and KMP details
- Meetings held
- Compliance certificate

Fee: Based on authorized capital
```

### Director Compliance

| Filing        | Due Date                    | Platform   | Notes                             |
| ------------- | --------------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------- |
| **DIR-3 KYC** | September 30                | mca.gov.in | Annual for all directors          |
| **MBP-1**     | First board meeting each FY | Internal   | Director's disclosure of interest |

```
DIR-3 KYC Required Documents:
- Aadhaar
- PAN
- Email
- Mobile
- Address proof

Process:
1. Log in to MCA portal with DIN
2. File DIR-3 KYC (Web or eForm)
3. OTP verification
4. Digital signature
5. Fee: ₹0 (before deadline), ₹5,000 (after deadline)
```

### Board Meetings

| Meeting           | Frequency | Requirement                           |
| ----------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Board Meeting** | Quarterly | At least 4 per year, gap ≤ 120 days   |
| **AGM**           | Annual    | Within 6 months of FY end (by Sep 30) |
| **EGM**           | As needed | For special resolutions               |

```
Board Meeting Requirements:
- Minimum 2 directors present (or 1/3, whichever higher)
- At least 1 independent director (if applicable)
- 7 days' notice required
- Minutes to be recorded within 30 days

AGM Requirements:
- All shareholders invited
- 21 days' clear notice
- Financial statements approval
- Director appointment/reappointment
- Auditor appointment
```

## RBI Compliance (For FDI)

### FC-GPR (Foreign Currency Transfer)

```
When: Within 30 days of share allotment to foreign investor
Form: FC-GPR
Portal: FIRMS (RBI)

Required for:
- Initial investment from US parent
- Any additional investment

Documents:
- Board resolution
- Share certificates
- Valuation report
- KYC of foreign investor
- FIRC (if remittance received)
```

### FLA Return (Annual)

```
Due: July 15
Portal: FIRMS (RBI)

Who files:
- Any company with foreign investment (FDI)
- Any company with overseas investment (ODI)

Contents:
- Foreign liabilities (share capital from abroad)
- Foreign assets (investments abroad)
- Intercompany loans
- Trade credits

Preparation:
- Get data from annual financial statements
- Coordinate with CA for accuracy
```

## Transfer Pricing Compliance

### Annual Requirements

| Requirement                   | Threshold             | Due Date                           |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Form 3CEB**                 | Transactions > ₹1 Cr  | By ITR due date                    |
| **TP Documentation**          | Transactions > ₹1 Cr  | Maintain, produce on request       |
| **Country-by-Country Report** | Group revenue > €750M | Not applicable for small companies |

```
For RR Tech Pvt Ltd:

If intercompany transactions exceed ₹1 Crore:
1. Maintain transfer pricing documentation
2. File Form 3CEB with tax return
3. Benchmark pricing with comparable companies

If transactions are below ₹1 Crore:
- No formal filing required
- Still maintain documentation as best practice
- Arm's length pricing still required
```

### Documentation to Maintain

```
1. Intercompany Agreement (Master Service Agreement)
2. Monthly invoices
3. Time sheets / work logs
4. Cost calculation worksheets
5. Markup justification
6. Benchmarking study (if transactions > ₹1 Cr)
```

## Penalty Reference

### US Penalties

| Violation                   | Penalty                    |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Late Delaware Franchise Tax | $200 + 1.5%/month          |
| Late Form 1120              | 5% of tax/month, max 25%   |
| Failure to file 1120        | $450/month for small corps |
| FinCEN BOI late             | $500/day, up to $10,000    |

### India Penalties

| Violation              | Penalty                                                 |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Late GST Return        | ₹50/day (CGST + SGST)                                   |
| Late Income Tax Return | ₹5,000 (₹1,000 if income < ₹5L)                         |
| Late DIR-3 KYC         | ₹5,000                                                  |
| Late ROC filings       | Additional fees + potential prosecution                 |
| Non-filing of FLA      | RBI scrutiny, potential issues with future transactions |
