## Organic Growth Channels

### 1. X/Twitter Growth

#### Profile Optimization
```

     @UnQuest | Open source enthusiast
Link: rakeshroushan.com
```

#### Content Strategy
```
Daily posting schedule:

Morning (8-10 AM):
- Insight/value tweet
- Thread (2-3x per week)

Afternoon (12-2 PM):
- Engagement replies
- Industry commentary

Evening (5-7 PM):
- Personal update
- Question/poll
- Building in public
```

#### Content Pillars
1. **Build in public** (40%) - Progress updates, metrics, learnings
2. **Educational** (30%) - Tips, tutorials, frameworks
3. **Personal** (20%) - Stories, opinions, behind-the-scenes
4. **Promotional** (10%) - Product launches, features

#### Growth Tactics
- Reply to large accounts (first 30 min)
- Use threads for complex topics
- Quote tweet with insights
- Host Twitter Spaces (monthly)
- Collaborate with similar creators

### 2. SEO & Content

#### Content Types

| Type | SEO Value | Effort | Frequency |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Comparison posts | High | Medium | Weekly |
| How-to guides | High | Medium | Weekly |
| Landing pages | High | Low | Per feature |
| Tool reviews | Medium | Low | Bi-weekly |
| Case studies | Medium | High | Monthly |

#### Keyword Strategy
```
Target keyword types:

1. Problem keywords
   "how to edit podcast faster"
   "market research for startups"

2. Competitor keywords
   "[competitor] alternative"
   "[competitor] vs [other]"

3. Solution keywords
   "ai podcast editor"
   "automated market research"

4. Long-tail keywords
   "best ai tool for solo podcasters"
   "free market research for indie hackers"
```

#### SEO Checklist per Article
- [ ] Primary keyword in title
- [ ] Primary keyword in first paragraph
- [ ] H2s with related keywords
- [ ] Internal links (3-5)
- [ ] External links (2-3 authoritative)
- [ ] Meta description with keyword
- [ ] Alt text on images
- [ ] 1500+ words for pillar content

### 4. Email Marketing

#### List Building
- Lead magnet on every product site
- Exit-intent popup
- Content upgrades in blog posts
- Free tool tier with email gate

#### Email Sequences

**Onboarding Sequence (5 emails)**
```
Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + quick start
Email 2 (Day 1): Core feature tutorial
Email 3 (Day 3): Success story or tip
Email 4 (Day 5): Advanced feature
Email 5 (Day 7): Check-in + support offer
```

**Nurture Sequence (Ongoing)**
```
Weekly: Value email (tips, content, updates)
Monthly: Product updates, new features
Quarterly: Big announcements, case studies
```

**Win-back Sequence**
```
Email 1 (Day 30 post-churn): "We miss you"
Email 2 (Day 45): "What we've improved"
Email 3 (Day 60): Final offer/discount
```

## Paid Acquisition (When Ready)

### When to Start Paid Ads

Start when:
- Organic brings steady signups
- CAC is understood
- LTV > 3x CAC achievable
- $1,000/month budget available

### Channel Priority

| Channel | Best For | Min Budget |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Google Ads | Intent-based search | $500/mo |
| Facebook/Instagram | B2C, visual products | $500/mo |
| LinkedIn | B2B, high LTV | $1,000/mo |
| Twitter Ads | Tech audience | $300/mo |
| Reddit Ads | Niche communities | $200/mo |

### Budget Allocation

```
Testing phase (Month 1):
- 50% Google Search
- 30% Facebook/Instagram
- 20% Twitter

Based on results, shift to top performer.
```

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| CAC | Total spend / Customers | < LTV/3 |
| ROAS | Revenue / Ad spend | > 3x |
| CPC | Spend / Clicks | < $2 |
| Conversion | Purchases / Clicks | > 2% |

| Integration partnerships | High | Medium |
| Co-marketing | Medium | Medium |

### Finding Partners

1. **Complementary products** - Products your users also use
2. **Adjacent audiences** - Same ICP, different problem
3. **Influencers** - People with your audience's trust
4. **Agencies** - Can resell to clients

## Retention & Expansion

### Reduce Churn

| Tactic | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| Onboarding | Email sequence + in-app guidance |
| Activation | First-value milestone celebration |
| Check-ins | 30/60/90 day automated emails |
| Win-backs | Re-engagement campaigns |
| Feedback | Exit surveys + improvement |

### Churn Triggers to Monitor

```
Warning signs (automate alerts):
- No login in 7 days (active user)
- Usage dropped 50%
- Failed payment
- Support complaints
- Feature requests ignored
```

### Expansion Revenue

| Strategy | How |
|----------|-----|
| Upsells | Prompt upgrade at usage limits |
| Cross-sells | Promote other RR products |
| Annual plans | Offer 2 months free |
| Team plans | Multi-seat discounts |
