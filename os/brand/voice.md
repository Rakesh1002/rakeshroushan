# Voice & style guide

## Voice principles

- **First principles > hot takes**
- **Frameworks + examples**
- **Clarity over cleverness**
- **Respect the reader’s time**

## Writing style

- Short paragraphs
- Bullets before prose
- Always end with: next action / question / takeaway

## Approved phrases (your “language”)

- “Here’s the model…”
- “The constraint is…”
- “The scoreboard is…”
- “If I had to bet, I’d bet on…”

## Avoid

- “10x”, “hustle”, vague motivation
- “AI will change everything” without specifics

## Content formats you’ll repeat

- 1-page playbook
- Checklist
- Decision memo (context → options → decision → owner/date)
- Postmortem
