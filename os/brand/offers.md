- Outcome: A working execution system — weekly cadence, prioritization framework, distribution playbook — tailored to their product and stage
- Format: 4-week sprint (1 hr/week call + async Slack support)
- Price range: $2,000-5,000 per sprint
- "Why now": AI product window is closing fast — the next 12 months decide who wins
- Proof needed: 2-3 founder testimonials + case study showing before/after shipping velocity
