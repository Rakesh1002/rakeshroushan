# Monthly CEO review (2–3 hours)

## Outcomes

- Review last month’s scoreboard (from `os/metrics/scoreboard.csv`)
- Pick the 3 biggest wins + why they happened
- Pick the 3 biggest misses + root cause

## Portfolio allocation

For each startup:
- Keep / pause / stop decision
- Next milestone + expected timeline
- CEO time allocation (hours/week)

## Strategy refresh (first principles)

- What is the most unfair advantage you have right now?
- What distribution channel is compounding?
- What capability must be built next (people/system/tech)?

## Content + brand

- Personal narrative (what are you known for now?)
- 3 content themes for next month

## Systems

- What friction happened repeatedly? → automate or redesign process
- Add automations under `os/automation/`
